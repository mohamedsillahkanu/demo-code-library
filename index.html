About Us

Informatics Consultancy Firm - Sierra Leone (ICF-SL) is dedicated to providing comprehensive and sustainable data-driven solutions for governmental and non-governmental organizations. Our mission is to facilitate informed decision-making processes, enhance data management capabilities, and promote data literacy within communities.

We believe that data is a vital tool for sustainable development. With our team of skilled professionals, we offer expertise in data management, capacity building, and analytical services that align with our client's objectives for impactful outcomes.
